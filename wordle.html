Wordle Game

Game Instraction:

Guess the Wordle Game in 6 tries.

Each guess must be a valid 5 letter word. Hit the Enterbutton to submit.

After each guess, the color of the titles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.


The letter color gray is not in the word in any spot.


The letter color yellow is in the word in the wrong spot.


The letter color green is in the word and in the correct spot.
